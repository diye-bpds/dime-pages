About


Overview

DIYE, an abbreviation for Do It Yourself, Efficiently will provide entrepreneurs lacking business plan development academic skills and knowledge with a business plan development system that allows them to freely and quickly develop their business plans themselves, rather than hiring business plan development consultants.

Characteristics

  • Accessibility - It is easy and free to acquire.
  • Simplicity - It is easy to understand and utilize.
  • Efficiency - It is less time consuming.
  • Familiarity - It conforms to accepted customs, rules and standards.
  • Professionalism - It produces high quality work.
